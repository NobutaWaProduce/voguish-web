F.E.A.R.

Fear. Enmity. Aversion. Revulsion.

F.E.A.R. was createdd in universe II by people who wanted to over throw the king of the land and create a perfect world. It was originally caled Freedom, but after Levi, Blu, and Rai took over they changed it to F.E.A.R, because the only way to truly keep control over people is through fear. They killed the oldd king andd took over his land. Some of the kings sons are working with them while others are going against them.

F.E.A.R. uses science and supernatural recourses in order to create their people. They do many exparaments on people, not caring if they live or die. If they live they are a sucess, if they die they are a failure. The rank system in F.E.A.R. is very elaborate, but simple once people undderstand. They hold a strict rule over people, punishing anyone that dares to break the rules that are given. If people want to gain status they must kill.

They began to take over other worlds when they found out that theirs were not the only one existed. They felt that they needed to bring order to all of the worlds. So they sent people to those worlds, spies. They told them how the people of those worlds worked and how they could take them overe, making it easier for F.E.A.R. to gain control. Some took time, others they stole in a day. There is no world safe from F.E.A.R.
