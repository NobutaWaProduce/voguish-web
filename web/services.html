The Keepers

The Keepers are the people who the king gave the journals too. When he gave them the journals they were all very young, being only the age of five. He felt that children would be more likely to create things of joy, but if they did not, if their life forced them do different things, then maybe the being that created their world would see the errors of his ways.

Each of the keeper did not have a kind life though. Each of them were obsessed with something. Insanity. Fear. Death. Those things affected the keepers so much their once pure minds because their greatest enemies.
