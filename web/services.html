The Keepers

The Keepers are the people who the king gave the journals too. When he gave them the journals they were all very young, being only the age of five. He felt that children would be more likely to create things of joy, but if they did not, if their life forced them do different things, then maybe the being that created their world would see the errors of his ways.

Each of the keeper did not have a kind life though. Each of them were obsessed with something. Insanity. Fear. Death. Those things affected the keepers so much their once pure minds because their greatest enemies.

Nothing was the first to be given a journal. He was an only child in a very wealthy family. He did not spend much time with his family though, he mainly spent time with his nanny. He was kidnapped by her one day and as a way to make him behave she threatened him with 'The blue monster in the corner'. He was so tramitized by this his parents sent him to therapy. One of his first creations was Blu, the actual blue monster in the corner. His family though that he was going insane when he cried about seeing the monster.

Echo was the second to be given a journal. She had two older sisters and one older brother. Her family was very happy until there was an incident one day. Things started going down hill.
