Neutral

There are people who do not like picking sides. They are the neutrals. They do not wish to be apart of any war or they do not like either side of the war. They choose to live their life normally, minding their own buisness and never getting into any trouble, or they do what ever they want, never following anyone's rules.

Most neutrals are angles, celestials, divine beings, and reapers. They follow strick rules that they are not allowed to break, but some of them find ways to join a side without breaking the rules. Rulers of kingdoms or groups are also mostly neutral, all they want is what is best for their people.
